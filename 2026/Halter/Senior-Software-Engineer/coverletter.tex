I am excited to apply for the \textbf{Senior Software Engineer — Go-to-Market} role at Halter. I'm comfortable integrating AI models and pipelines thoughtfully into well-designed systems, and I'm actively deepening my work with vector databases, embeddings and retrieval so I can help define how AI capabilities get built across a product.

I'm not precious about where a problem lives — frontend, backend, or the data pipeline in between — I just want to solve it well and bring the team along with me. I thrive in fast-paced, ambiguous environments where I get to own meaningful slices of the product and iterate quickly with cross-functional teams.

Finally, I'm genuinely energised by Halter's office-first culture and \textbf{ready to relocate to Auckland} to be in the room with the team. Thank you for your time and consideration — I would love the chance to help build the AI-powered surfaces that Halter's customers, support and commercial teams depend on every day.
